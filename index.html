Top Attractions in Rhodes

  • Palaia Poli (Old Town)

    Explore the medieval charm of the Old Town, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

  • Butterfly Valley

    A peaceful natural reserve filled with butterflies during summer.

  • Acropolis of Lindos

    An ancient archaeological site with stunning views of the Aegean Sea.

  • Kallithea Springs

    Historical thermal baths with stunning architecture and views.

  • Monolithos Castle

    A medieval castle offering breathtaking views of the coastline.

Beautiful Beaches in Rhodes

  • Tsambika Beach

    A sandy beach with crystal-clear waters, perfect for families.

  • Anthony Quinn Bay

    A picturesque bay named after the famous actor, ideal for snorkeling.

  • Prasonisi

    A unique spot where the Aegean and Mediterranean seas meet.

  • Faliraki Beach

    A lively beach with water sports and vibrant nightlife.

  • Agathi Beach

    A hidden gem with golden sand and shallow waters.
